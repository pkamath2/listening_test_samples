Project Details:

The purpose of this research is to evaluate the quality of synthetic audio clips generated using an artificial neural network. The audio clips under test belong to the domain of audio textures (for e.g. a series of popping sounds, the ticking sound made by a Geiger counter or the sound made by water filling a container) or pitches made by musical instruments. The specific goal of this project is to ensure that the synthetic outputs generated by these deep learning models are validated by human beings to test for certain timbral qualities.
